How we source

Six farms. One standard. No compromises.

FreeFly works with six multi-generational BC farms in the Fraser Valley and Okanagan. Each farm is audited twice yearly by an independent third party. The audit covers land use, feed sourcing, welfare indicators, and antibiotic records.

When a farm fails an audit, we pause the relationship. We have done it before. The short-term supply disruption is cheaper than the long-term reputational cost of shipping a compromised product to a restaurant that trusts us.
